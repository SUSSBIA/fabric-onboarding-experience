# Workspace Health Note

Use this template to summarise the health of a Fabric workspace.

This note is useful when reviewing a sandbox workspace, department workspace, or production candidate workspace. It helps capture workspace purpose, access, ownership, data sensitivity, refresh, operational risks, and follow-up actions.
